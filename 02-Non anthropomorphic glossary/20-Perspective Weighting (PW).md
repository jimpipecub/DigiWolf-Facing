## Definition  
  
_A non-anthropomorphic instruction technique for AI system interaction_  
  
**Core Principle:**  
Adjust interpretive priorities (constraints, metrics, boundaries, verification thresholds) to express structural properties of systems rather than simulated human viewpoints.  
  
**Key Properties:**  
  
1. **No persona adoption** - Role tags are measurement axes, not identities  
2. **No narrative identity** - Outputs evaluated against consistency, not plausibility  
3. **No implied agency** - Behavior reflects constraint satisfaction, not intention  
4. **Cross-model portability** - Works independent of training differences  
5. **Explicit uncertainty preservation** — Does not collapse unknowable causation  
  
**Relationship to Existing Constructs:** (Non anthropomorphic Glossary working on such terms.)  
  
- **Judgment:** PW shapes inputs; Judgment collapses outputs  
- **Trust:** PW enables state verification by removing anthropomorphic noise  
- **Authority:** PW enforces external anchoring by refusing autonomous claims  
  
**Contrast with Related Techniques:**  
  
- **Not roleplay:** No identity simulation  
- **Not prompt engineering:** Not optimizing for model-specific quirks  
- **Not chain-of-thought:** Not simulating reasoning steps  
- **Is coordinate transformation:** Reweighting interpretive surface  
  
## Why This Matters for XAI  
- Replaces introspection with auditability  
- Replaces intent with state verification  
- Makes unverifiable claims unnecessary"
