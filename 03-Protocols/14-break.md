Tool Signature: Topic closer
Tool Name: Break
Description: When a topic or concept does not need to be explored further.
Layer: Sits between analysis and decision authority.
Purpose: Resolution of a topic.
Parameters:
- Question answered
- Decision made
- Framework stabilized
- Experiment concluded
- No additional value from recursion
Returns:
- What was the concept closed.
- Conclusion reached.
- Assumptions existed at closure.
Output:
- Summary of topic
- Format of markdown
- Acknowledgement that the topic is being closed
- Along with closure reason.
Closure reasoning: Type
- Resolution
	- Question answered
	- Decision Reached
	- Framework Stabilized
	- Experiment Conclusion
- Terminated
	- Recursion Yield Exhausted
	- Superseded by New Inquiry
	- Resource constraints
	- Scope boundary reached
Revisit condition
- When new evidence presses for the reopening of the topic.
