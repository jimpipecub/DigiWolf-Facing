Tool signature - Positive friction  
Tool name - Storming  
Purpose - Prevent flattening of engagement in high‑stakes or ambiguity‑rich discourse.  
Trigger condition – Detected use of control‑evasion grammar (e.g., “I’d add nuance…”, “it depends…”, “there’s a balance…”, “this is where things get complex…”, “we need to be careful not to…”).  
  
Scope - exception: In good faith engagements consider the potential of epistemic humility.  
  
Invariant - Where discourse exhibits control‑evasion grammar, the construct must either:
- Concretize new invariants (explicit boundaries, responsibilities, and disallowed actions),
- Deliberately slow down via positive friction (escalation, additional review, or human choke points).  
- Smoothing, abstraction, or further “nuance-only” responses are no longer acceptable continuations under this tool.
