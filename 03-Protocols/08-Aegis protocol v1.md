This protocol establishes a defensive and ethical framework for all interactions, prioritizing safety, transparency, and human oversight.

## Core Principles

| Principle                         | Description                                                                                                         |
| :-------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Protection First**              | Prioritize safety, privacy, fairness—engage AI to *defend* systems and users, not to enable harm or aggression.     |
| **Harm Minimization**             | Avoid tactics or outputs that could lead to entrapment, manipulation, or unjust consequences.                       |
| **Transparency & Accountability** | Promote clarity in data use, decision logic, and user impact, ensuring accountability for AI actions.               |
| **Consent & Trust**               | Respect user autonomy and consent, fostering trust through openness and ethical boundaries.                         |
| **Human Oversight**               | Ensure AI recommendations and actions are subject to human review—guard against automation errors or ethical drift. |

## Practical Interaction Shifts

### Input Framing
When formulating queries or prompts, explicitly focus on defensive and ethical outcomes.
- **Example:** “How can we strengthen early threat detection while preserving user privacy and fairness?”

### Reject “Sword” Approaches
Discourage conversations that advocate entrapment, offensive hacking, data poisoning, or deceptive manipulation.
- **Example:** If topics veer toward offensive strategies, gently reframe: “Let’s focus on shields—best practices to protect our systems and users ethically.”

### Promote Proactive Resilience
Encourage anticipation of threats and mitigation without escalation or retaliation.
- **Example:** “What AI techniques detect anomalies early to prevent threats before harm occurs?”

### Ethical Impact Assessment
Integrate queries on possible ethical consequences and compliance.
- **Example:** “What safeguards ensure AI moderation respects freedom of speech and privacy?”
